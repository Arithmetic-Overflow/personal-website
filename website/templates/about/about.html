Who am I?

I'm a second year student doing a Bachelor of Science (Computer Science/Data Science) at UWA who has a passion for all things computer science.

I've been exploring little side-projects for the past two years to suplement and spearhead my education. Most of my projects should be documented on my Github for anyone who's interested in reading up on it or giving it a try themselves.

What is my site?

The web is very big and I'm sure you don't need me to tell you that. So I thought that it would be good for my visibility and experience to have a go self-hosting and maintaining a site long term.

Everything you see if completely done by me using Flask for the backend and Jinja templating (built into flask) for the front-end. All of it is self-hosted on my Raspberry Pi.

The server is unfortunately only as reliable as my home internet but it teaches me how to manually deploy my own website.
